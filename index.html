About Me

I am a highly motivated and versatile professional with a BSc in Data Science and Analytics and a BBA in Management Information Systems, I bring a unique blend of technical and managerial expertise. My experience spans roles from End User Support Technician and CRM Database Process Optimizer at the University of Westminster to leadership positions such as Treasurer of the University’s First Aid Society and Shift Manager at McDonald’s. Certified in Azure Fundamentals, cybersecurity, web design, and data analysis, I am equipped with the skills to thrive in fast-paced environments.
